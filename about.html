Kara Rehman is a product designer based in Brooklyn, New York.

She designs for people who want to build deeper relationships with the content and creators they love and knows how to translate that into product patterns people keep coming back to. Most recently, she worked at Vox Media on the Consumer Revenue team, where she focused on conversion, retention, and loyalty for New York Magazine's subscriber experience. She also teaches interaction design at Parsons School of Design.

When she's not looking at a screen, she likes making things with her hands in the form of cooking, knitting, and sewing.
